Why Choose Service Robot?

Service robots are designed for specific tasks, such as cleaning and manufacturing. They offer benefits such as increased efficiency, improved safety, and cost savings. They can work autonomously or be remotely controlled, and can handle tasks that are dangerous or repetitive for humans.

  • Service robots assist human beings, typically by performing a job that is dirty, dull, distant, dangerous or repetitive.
  • The primary benefits that end users see from professional service robots is increased productivity, like many other forms of automation. Professional service robots can automate tasks that were previously impossible to automate. The connected nature of these robots allows for detailed data collection that facilitates ongoing productivity improvement.
  • Professional service robots have far greater uptime, speed, and consistency when compared to manual labor, leading to major gains in efficiency. By automating dangerous or strenuous tasks, these robots also introduce greater safety into a wide range of applications.
