About Us

  • The idea is initiated by Mohit Nitin Motani in 2023.
  • Robowale main head quartered in Mumbai
  • Mr. Mohit Nitin Motani has been in the business of Service Robots for the last 8 years.
  • He is a Robotic Engineer from University of Leeds, UK with a Master’s Degree in AI and IoT from MIT, USA and a business degree in Corporate Strategic Management and International Marketing from Harvard Business School, USA.
  • Robowale has started with a capital of Rs. 10 lacs and 2 Robots
  • The reason behind starting Robowale is to bring Service Robots at an affordable price point in India and show businesses and people how Robots can add value to their daily lives in every possible aspect they want it to.
