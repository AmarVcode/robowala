SWOT ANALYSIS

What is SWOT?

SWOT analysis is a strategic planning tool used by organizations to identify and evaluate their strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. It involves conducting an in-depth analysis of an organization's internal and external factors that affect its performance and competitiveness.

Strengths

→ Robowale has a first mover advantage
→ The company is very well adapted with the Service Robots, changing trends, software and hardware upgrades and serviceability of Service Robots

Weaknesses

→ At this stage, managing customer expectation, and executing with 99% satisfaction is a big challenge.
→ Competitors over-commit and under-deliver, Robowale believes in under-commitment and over-deliver.

Opportunities

→ Scalability – There is a huge opportunity for Robowale to scale to all cities across India and globally

Threats

→ The greatest threat are other robot white label brands that might want to start something similar, the key to which is to scale fast and build a household name with strong brand recall.

PESTEL ANALYSIS

What is PESTEL?

PESTEL analysis is a tool used by organizations to assess external factors that may impact their business. It considers political, economic, social, technological, environmental, and legal factors. This analysis helps organizations make informed strategic decisions by understanding industry trends. PESTEL factors include government policies, macroeconomic conditions, demographics, technological advancements, sustainability, and legal regulations.

Political

→ As Service Robots operate under electrical machinery, there is no special permissions required to operate the Robots.
→ Digital India push has played a crucial role in promoting Service Robots in India.

Economic

→ Battery and electronics prices have been a major influential factor. As more and more batteries and electronic components are being made in India, the price of robots are getting better.
→ GST is 18% on Robot sales as well as RaaS.

Sociol-Cultural

→ Service Robots have everyone’s eye and attraction. It is an excellent tool to connect, guide and get feedback from individuals.
→ Youth are in love for technology, and Robots are the ultimate testimony to this.

Technological

→ Robot diagnostics is constantly monitored on our service platforms.

Environmental

→ Robots are 100% environmental friendly as there is zero emission in operating them.
→ In some cases it is also observed where using robots reduces carbon footprint if same task was done using traditional methods.

Legal
